## Description of dataset:
Our dataset focuses on recalls of food and dietary supplements and was obtained from the official FDA website (https://www.fda.gov/safety/recalls-market-withdrawals-safety-alerts). The dataset contains 898 rows and 7 columns, providing detailed information about recalled products and the reasons behind those recalls. It includes a variety of dimensions with different data types that allow for meaningful analysis. Specifically, the dataset includes the date the recall was initiated (date & time), the brand name associated with the product (string), a detailed product description (string), the product type such as food, drugs, or cosmetics (string), the reason for the recall such as contamination or mislabeling (string), the company responsible for the product (string), and whether the recall has been terminated or is still ongoing (categorical). Overall, this dataset contains a mix of categorical and time-based variables, enabling analysis of recall trends over time as well as comparisons across product types, companies, and recall reasons, which helps provide insight into patterns related to food safety and public health risks.
